How to Access Your Attendance Record in Orison

Step 1: Go to the Login Page

Open your web browser and navigate to your school's Orison portal login page. It will look similar to the image below.

Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

Enter your assigned User Name and Password into the respective fields. Click the "Log in" button to proceed.

Step 3: Navigate the Dashboard

After logging in, you will land on the main dashboard. Locate the navigation menu on the left side of the screen.

Step 4: Open the Academics Menu

In the left-side navigation menu, find and click on the "Academics" section. This will expand a sub-menu with more options.

Step 5: Select Absence Report

From the Academics sub-menu, click on "Absence Report". This will take you to your detailed attendance page.

Step 6: View Your Attendance Record

You can now see your complete attendance record. This page shows key information like Total Leave and Working Days, which you can use in the calculator below.

Attendance Calculator

Use the values from your "Absence Report" to calculate your current standing.
